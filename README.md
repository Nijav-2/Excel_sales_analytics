# 📊 Sales Analytics Project

## 📌 Overview  
This project focuses on performing detailed **sales data analysis** using Microsoft Excel to understand product performance, customer behavior, and regional sales distribution. The objective is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that support business decision-making.

All reports and dashboards created for this project are **exported and presented in PDF format** for easy access and sharing.

---

## 🎯 Objectives  
- Analyze product-wise sales performance  
- Identify top and low-performing products  
- Understand customer contribution to overall sales  
- Evaluate sales distribution across cities and stores  
- Generate actionable business insights  

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed  

### 📦 Product Analysis  
- Quantity sold for each product  
- Top 10 performing products  
- Bottom 10 performing products  

### 👥 Customer Analysis  
- Top 5 customers based on sales  
- Bottom 5 customers based on sales  
- Customer contribution to total revenue  

### 🌍 Geographical Analysis  
- Total net sales by cities  
- Regional performance comparison  

### 🏬 Sales Channel Analysis  
- Total sales by stores  
- Identification of high and low-performing stores  

---

- Data Cleaning & Transformation  
- Sorting & Filtering
